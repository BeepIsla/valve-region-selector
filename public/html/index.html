Help & Information
Simple usage guide
  1. Close all games
  2. Select the game you want to play
  3. Choose your fake ping for each region
  4. (Optional) You can disable regions which will use your real ping and not modify them
  5. Click Enable
  6. Select your Steam.exe and wait until it opens
  7. Run your game and play it
Note: Keep this program open, closing it will kill your Steam process and cause issues.
Tips
  • You can modify the pings of multiple games at once without having to restart to switch game.
  • You can modify the pings while this program is already enabled. No restarting required!
